How it works?

loopIToy is a platform where you can also easily sell toys that are not interesting for your kids anymore.

Register

How to sell toys?

Drop us pictures of toys via form. Wait for our approval to send us toys. In case of more than 10 toys, we offer free delivery.

Which toys can you sent us?

Our e-shop focus mainly on toys produced from sustainable or eco-friendly material. However, we accept also other toys if they support the child development, like montesorry toys. We take books and puzzles too. The toys should be fully functional - although we repair them if needed for a reasonably fee.

How are the toys sold?

The toys are on e-shop for 12 months. The price is derived based on the usage level and original price. If the toy is not sold within 4 months we start reducing the price.

Once the toy is sold, you get 50% of the sell.
